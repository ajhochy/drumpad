## Version Information (1.0 / Build 1)

**Description (4000 chars max — ~1700 chars used):**

drumrot is a drum trainer built like an SP-808 groove box. Hit the pads in time with the scrolling highway, rack up combos, and unlock a collection of bizarre drum-machine characters called drumrots.

**Play tab** — A note highway scrolls toward the strike line. Six lane-colored rubber pads map to kick, snare, hi-hat, crash, tom, and ride. Hit early and you'll see EARLY. Hit late and you'll see LATE. Hit right and the COMBO ticks up. Miss and it resets.

**Library tab** — A growing set of rhythm lessons sorted by genre and difficulty. Tap any card to load it into the Play tab. Your best accuracy and star rating are saved per lesson.

**Builder tab** — A 6×16/32 step sequencer. Tap cells to build your own groove, dial in the BPM, and load it into the Play tab for a real scoring session. Export your pattern as a standard MIDI file to use in any DAW.

**Progress tab** — Streak calendar, session stats, and an achievement grid. Achievements unlock drumrot drops: collect them all.

**Drops tab** — Your drumrot collection. Each drumrot is a unique drum-machine character with its own personality. Rare OG variants have holofoil effects and infinite max stats.

**Hardware MIDI:** Connect any class-compliant USB-C or Lightning-to-USB MIDI controller and the pads respond to incoming notes (GM drum map). Network MIDI and Bluetooth MIDI are also supported. The app makes a great companion to electronic drum kits.

drumrot is an original app by Visalia CRC. All drumrot characters are original parody designs. No data is collected.

## App Review Information

**Sign-in required:** No

**Notes for App Review:**
The main gameplay loop is on the Play tab — select any lesson from the Library
tab, then press Play on the Play tab. The pads (bottom of screen, landscape
orientation) trigger drum sounds and are scored by the highway. No MIDI
controller is required; the on-screen pads exercise the full feature set.

The Builder tab lets you create a custom groove with the step sequencer and
load it into Play. The Drops tab shows the character collection earned through
gameplay. The Progress tab shows streaks and achievements.

The app is iPad-only and landscape-locked. Bluetooth entitlement is present for
BLE MIDI pairing (optional hardware feature); the app works fully without it.
Local Network entitlement is present for Network MIDI (optional); no server,
no data collection.
